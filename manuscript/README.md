# Manuscript directory

## Canonical manuscript

`MANUSCRIPT_THEORETICAL_ECOLOGY.md` is the sole active manuscript source.

It currently contains:

- title;
- abstract and keywords;
- cited Introduction;
- model definitions and sign criterion;
- mechanism non-identifiability proposition;
- environmental comparative statics;
- finite sensitivity-analysis methods;
- results based on the canonical endpoint-normalized V2 run;
- Discussion and Conclusions;
- declarations and references.

The former standalone `INTRODUCTION_DRAFT.md` was merged into the canonical manuscript and removed to prevent divergent manuscript versions.

## Submission-support documents

Journal strategy, cover letter, and upload checklist are maintained under `submission/`.

## Scientific boundary

The manuscript has one primary contribution: a focal-pair local diagnostic for one declared floral attraction trait, one declared flower-specific defence or access-limitation trait, and one declared outcome scale.

The following are not manuscript claims:

- empirical prevalence of complementarity or substitutability;
- trait covariance, genetic correlation, optima, or evolutionary trajectories;
- validation of the regime map by the abstract-level literature registry;
- a general claim that antagonist pressure always favours complementarity;
- a new mathematical category distinct from established cross-trait fitness curvature.

## Remaining author-controlled fields

Before portal submission, the authors must supply and approve:

- author names, order, affiliations, and corresponding-author details;
- ORCID identifiers;
- CRediT contributions;
- funding and grant identifiers;
- competing-interest confirmation;
- acknowledgements;
- suggested and opposed reviewers;
- archival repository DOI and release identifier.
