## Why this is a B-path lead

The discovery metadata describes experimental florivory and early-season floral
anthocyanin and condensed-tannin measures associated with floral antagonist
outcomes. These chemical traits are plausible resistance candidates, but their
role as `B_flower` is not assumed from the abstract.

## Relationship to the existing 2018 Impatiens panel

The same plant species and overlapping investigators do not make this an
automatic duplicate of Gorden and Adler (2018). The 2016 paper is retained as a
separate source until its methods, experimental units, and reported outcomes are
screened. It must not be counted as an independent replication merely because it
is a separate publication.
